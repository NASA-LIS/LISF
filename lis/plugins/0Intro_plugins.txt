!The plugin interfaces in LIS are the functional abstractions to
!represent various user-defined components in LIS. The plugins contain
!the hot spots or extensible interfaces for incorporating new domains,
!running modes, LSMS, meteorological analyses, data assimilation
!tools, and parameters.  These adaptable interfaces in LIS enable
!the reuse of the broad set of data, high performance computing,
!data management, visualization tools, and the land modeling
!infrastructure in LIS.
