!\section{WRF AK}
!The WRF AK (Alaska) domain forcing data produced at NCAR by E. Gutmann, K. Ikeda, and 
!C. Liu (ref.: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00382-016-3327-9).
!These WRF-generated files are referred to by the domain, which is the state of Alaska,
!and are post-processed into daily files.
!The dataset is on polar stereographic grid at 4km resolution, encompassing most
!of the Alaska peninsula, and available at 1-hour intervals.
!EOP
