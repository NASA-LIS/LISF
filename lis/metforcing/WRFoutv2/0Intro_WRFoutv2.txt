!\section{WRFoutv2}
!The WRFout version 2 forcing data produced at NCAR by E. Gutmann, K. Ikeda, and 
!C. Liu (ref.: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00382-016-3327-9).
!These WRF-generated files are referred here as Version 2, and are post-processed into 
!annual files (i.e., calendar years) per each required forcing field.
!The dataset is on Lambert conic conformal grid at 4km resolution, encompassing most
!of North America, and available at 1-hour intervals.
!EOP
